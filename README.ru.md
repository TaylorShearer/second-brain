A full-featured Second Brain with a built-in Google Workspace module (Gog). Everything runs **completely locally** on your device — no cloud, no subscriptions, with deep self-evolution.

### 🔥 Why thousands of people already choose Second Brain

- **Complete privacy** — nothing goes to the cloud
- **Deep Zettelkasten** with automatically evolving MOCs
- **Gog Workspace** — intelligent analysis of Gmail, Calendar, Drive, and Docs directly from screenshots
- **Two self-improvement systems** — GEP-lite + Personal Self-Improving Layer
- **Proactive evolution** — the skill analyzes your history itself and suggests improvements
- **User Correction Loop** — instantly learns from your edits
- **Long-term Memory** — remembers your preferences for 30–60 days
- **Interactive Evolution Map** — a beautiful dashboard showing the skill’s evolution

- Device with **12 GB RAM or higher** (recommended)

- **Gemma-4-E4B-it** model (3.6 GB)

### 🚀 Quick Installation (3 minutes)

1. Open **Google AI Edge Gallery** on your device.
2. Go to **Agent Skills** → **Add Custom Skill** → **From URL**.
3. Paste the link: `https://github.com/uussnn/second-brain`
4. Select the **Gemma-4-E4B-it** model.
5. Type in the chat: `activate second brain`

**Done!** The skill will immediately start working and learning for you.

*(Alternatively: download the repository as a [ZIP archive](https://github.com/uussnn/second-brain/archive/refs/heads/main.zip) and import the local `second-brain` folder.)*

### ⚡ Quick Commands

**📊 Reviews and Planning**

- `“day review”` — full analysis of today
- `“week review”` — summaries and plans
- `“what do I have today”` — quick overview of tasks and meetings

**🧠 Zettelkasten**

- `“MOC review”` — check and improve maps of content
- `“show MOC [topic]”` — open the relevant MOC
- `“update MOCs”` — automatically update all MOCs

**🗂 Search and Memory**

- `“what did I say about X”` — find all notes on a topic
- `“show my preferences”` — look into the Personal Layer

**🧬 Evolution**

- `“show dashboard”` — open the Evolution Map
- `“personal review”` — start a personal review
- `“compress memory”` — optimize the cache

**📧 Gog Workspace**

- `“analyze email”` — analyze important emails
- `“what’s on my calendar”` — meetings and events
- `“save to Second Brain”` — transfer data
